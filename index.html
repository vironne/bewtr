NOT A CHATBOT. A SUPER-EMPLOYEE.

The agent isn't a tool your team learns to use. It's a coworker that does its share — owns a workflow end-to-end, inside your existing tools, without supervision.

It works for your employees, never instead of them. It writes and sends, fills the CRM, generates and publishes, monitors and alerts. It removes the time that should never have been theirs.

The product isn't software, isn't a platform, isn't a license. The product is the agent itself — built, deployed and operated for your team.
